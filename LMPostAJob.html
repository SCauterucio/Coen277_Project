Frequently Asked Questions
Who can see my job post?
We'll share your job across LinkedIn and through email to professionals whose skills and location match the job. Anyone on LinkedIn can find your post through search, and if you have a Company Page, it will appear there, too. You can also share your job via social media.
Will I get a copy of the receipt?
You'll get an email receipt and you can also print your billing history from your Settings page.
How long will my job post be active, and can I renew it?
Job posts are live for 30 days once they're posted. You can renew your job at any time, which will extend the expiration date by 30 days and update the posting date to the renewal date. Renewals cost the same as a new job post.
Can I close my job post if I fill the position before the job post expires?
Yes, you can close your job post at any time before it expires. It will then be removed from search results, and while it can't be reopened, it can be reposted.
Can I edit my job post after it has been posted?
Yes, once a job is posted, you can make edits to it from the Manage Jobs page. You can change any field, except for the location, which can't be changed once it's posted.
How will I get an applicant's information when they apply for my job?
When you post a job, you can select how you want to receive applicant information. You can collect it on LinkedIn and get email updates with new applicants. Or, you can route applicants to an external website to apply, such as your career site or ATS.
